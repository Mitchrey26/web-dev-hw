David Berman is a singer, song writer, and a poet. Berman is mainly known for his music, performing in the band Silver Jews, Purple Mountains and Pavement. He also has written two poetry books, and a couple full length novels. The two poetry books that Berman has written are Actual Air and The Portabale February. Many of his songs could also stand alone as poems on their own, American Water is his most critically acclaimed album. David Berman was born in 1967 and passed away in August later year after releasing a new record called All My Happiness is Gone, he passed away only a few months of releasing the record.
