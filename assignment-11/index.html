The Prolific Singer Song Writer

(Sandy) Alex G is the stage alias of Alex Giannascoli, a singer, songwriter, and guitarist who built his reputation with intimate lo-fi pop that combines strong melodic sensibilities with a ragged, impulsive-sounding performance style. He is also known for diversions into recording experiments and noise.
