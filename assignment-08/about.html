Why I Wanted to Share My Project

Photography is imporatnt to me because you can capture anything that feels important to you, and you have it forever to remember. Photography can bring you back to the feelings, sounds, smells, and the exact momement. Night photgraphy and stars are fun to capture as the night sky is arguably one of the most buetiful scenes on the earth, no night sky looks the same. This is especially true with the photgraphs of the night. When you mess around with your new mount and camera, you will find that the longer the exposure the brighter the sky, focus has to be exactly right, ISO can change the detail of the sky. There are so many different ways to go about making an image that is meaningful to you. So, tonight get your camera out, and mess around with your new mount and camera, and create something meaningful to you.

Timelapses can last all night, and you can end up with stunning images of the night sky!
